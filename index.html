MAHABALESHWAR


  • LOCATION:

    Mahabaleshwar is located on the mountainous Sahyadri range of the Western ghats that run North to south along the western coast of India.

  • TOURISM :

    Mahabaleshwar is a major place of tourism in Maharashtra. Attractions include many hill side look out points with views of surrounding hills, valleys and forests such as Bombay Point, Arthur Seat, Kates Point, Lodwick-Wilson Point, and Elphinston Point. Wilson point is the only location in Mahabaleshwar where both sunrise and sunset can be seen. The town also has a man made lake dating back to British era called Venna Lake. The lake is a popular for boating.
